## What does this document cover?

This document covers the Past to Future Portal and Past to Future API. 

## What data do we collect?

For all users we collect Portal and API usage through our website logs. Logs are retained for a month. 

For the Past to Future project, we have the email addresses of project users, and through authenticated usage of the portal and API we track uploads, edits, deletions, and some downloads. We also store some information regarding the authentication and authorization process. 

## How long is data stored?

Logs are kept for a maximum of 30 days. 

Activity data is kept for a maximum of 90 days. 

## Tokens

Past to Future project members can request a token to add, update, remove, and read data on the portal and API. Tokens last 24 hours before expiring. 

## How can I exercise my rights regarding the GDPR?

Email the Past to Future project organizers [p2f@uu.nl](mailto:p2f@uu.nl)
